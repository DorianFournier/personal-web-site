About me

My name is Dorian Fournier and I’m 23. I’m currently in the fourth year of an Engineering program in Robotics and Embedded Systems at Bordeaux Ynov Campus. Before that, I graduated with a scientific A-Level and I followed two years of a Bachelor’s Degree in « Physics, Chemistry and Engineering Science » (PCSI) at Lyon Faculty of Science.

Passionate about new technologies and IT, I would like to specialize in embedded software development. Entrepreneurship and management are also skills that I would like to develop during my career as a project manager in software / embedded systems.

As a hobby, I like practicing snooker, golf and participating in some projects which use embedded programming or/and electronic.

I am currently working as an apprentice in the SII group - CES (Continental Engineering Services) division, since October 2021.
